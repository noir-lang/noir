Enum FieldKind

pub(crate) enum FieldKind {
    Tagged(u8),
    Skipped,
}
Expand description

What the macro should do with a given field on the wire.

Variants§

§

Tagged(u8)

Field appears on the wire under integer tag tag.

§

Skipped

Field is omitted from the wire (via explicit #[tag(skip)] or because its type is PhantomData<_>). Skipped fields contribute no entry to TAGS, no recursion into register_into, and no where-clause bound.
