Noir’s HIR (High-Level Intermediate Representation) is produced by the elaboration pass and represents a fully resolved and type-checked program. The HIR is the input to the monomorphization pass.

Information about how the AST is transformed into an HIR can be found in the elaborator module.

Monomorphization will take in the the HIR produced by elaboration and convert it to a monomorphized IR.
